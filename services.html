Pre Construction

West Fork Construction works closely with the owners, architects, and trade contractors during the pre-construction phase to assist and improve on the design and construction of the project. Our company strongly believes that the hard work and thorough analysis provided during this stage of the project will reduce cost and expedite schedules during the construction phase.

Preconstruction services offered include:
  • Review and analysis of materials
  • Building systems and equipment
  • Construction feasibility
  • Ongoing cost estimates and analysis of the different designs
  • Constructability Reviews
  • Budget Estimates
  • Value Analysis and Scheduling
  • Project Research
  • Subcontractor Selection
  • MEP Design

#2

Construction Services

During the construction phase, West Fork Construction uses several different systems and practices to manage the project. In addition to the original milestone schedule, we use a 6-week look ahead schedule. At times when a high level of detail and coordination is required, a 3-week or even 1-week schedule is used. Communication is the key to an accelerated schedule. Weekly planning meetings are held with the trade contractors to review and discuss the work.

To control the budget and keep the owner informed at all times, a Potential Change Order Log is kept current and reviewed with the ownerâ€™s representative. Throughout the entire process we work extensively with Trade Contractors to save cost and time on the project.

Construction services offered include:
  • Remodel projects
  • Extensive core
  • Shell building projects
  • Tenant improvement build

#3

Post Construction Services

West Fork Construction provides a full range of post construction support, training, documentation and a standard 12 month warranty for all work we've performed. We stand by the belief that our word is our bond, and we depend upon complete customer satisfaction to ensure our reputation gets us the next job.

In summary, we perform project follow-up prior to and after we've turned it over for occupation, and always maintain an open line of communication for any questions or concerns by:

  • 1.Developing a detailed schedule of close-out activities with the Owner that includes punch lists, equipment testing, start-up procedures, and occupancy. Incorporating this schedule into the Master Schedule and distribute it to all Project team members as appropriate.
  • 2.Scheduling and performing inspections to develop punch lists with all Subcontractors and the Owner. Establishing dates of Substantial and Final Completion.
  • 3.Organizing and coordinating with Subcontractors and the Owner to ensure/confirm delivery of all keys, manuals, warranties, as-built drawings, plans and specifications, lien releases, attic stock materials. Providing guidance to those performing maintenance and upkeep.
  • 4.Working with Subcontractors and verifying for the Owner that all punch list items are completed. Verifying Subcontractor payment and release of retainage for all invoices and obtain/distribute to Owner all lien releases.
  • 5.Coordinating with Subcontractors / Architects / Engineers to confirm compliance with specifications. Ensuring any deficiencies are corrected.

The end of construction is not the end of our relationship. We continue to serve our Owners to make sure all aspects of our project are satisfied. Our post construction services are the bridge to our next project.
