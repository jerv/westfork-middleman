Project Superintendent

Project Superintendent

West Fork Construction is a Sacramento based general contractor looking to add an experienced Commercial Construction Project Superintendent to our team to deliver successful projects and help us continue our growth into the future. With our honest, collaborative and straight forward approach, we have repeatedly earned the trust of our clientele. This approach and work ethic has allowed us to perform negotiated projects for our clients throughout our history.

West Fork Construction has 35 years, proven, track record of successful projects. Our diverse project experience includes: retail, commercial office buildings, parking structures, mixed-use, multi-family and healthcare. With a long list of ongoing and completed large and small remodel projects and extensive core and shell building projects, tenant improvement build, we maintain a steady stream of work and backlog. These projects range in size from <$1M to $25M+.

West Fork Construction is seeking and individual who has 10+ years of experience successfully completing commercial projects in the environment described above. This individual would possess the following core values:

Strong work ethic:

Absolute dedication, honoring commitments and willingness to put in the extra effort to ensure client satisfaction and project success.

Teamwork:

Collaborative spirit, sharing ideas and talents with team members, subcontractors, architects and owners. Support others when needed and ask for help and support when required.

Communication:

Honest, straight forward, clear, open communicator. Actively listen to and be respectful of other people’s ideas and points of view.

Integrity:

Deliver a quality project experience as promised. Possess high business and personal ethics. Treat all people fairly and with honesty.

Key Responsibilities:

The Project Superintendent has the primarily responsibility for all field operations of the project. The Project Superintendent will be responsible for and/or participate in the following activities:

  • Provide leadership to all field personnel, subcontractors and suppliers to accomplish and complete all aspects of the project safely, efficiently and effectively.
  • Provide site management, coordination, and ongoing inspections of workmanship while the project is in progress. Provide a safe, clean and professional environment at all times during the construction of the project.
  • Regularly conduct construction subcontractor meetings. Prepare, maintain and distribute weekly 3-week project schedules that track with the overall project master schedule. Participate in planning and coordination sessions for specific activities. Maintain accurate minutes for all meetings.
  • Maintain project management and field required logs and documents (RFIs, change orders, addendums, submittals, as-builts, MSDS, IIPP, safety meetings, etc.). Keep all construction documents up to date with all changes and clarifications throughout the project.
  • Communicate regularly to keep the Project Manager and office staff up to date with all progress, field conditions, problems and incidents.
  • Provide assistance as required to the Project Manager for schedule updates, submittal progress, material procurement, estimating and any field administration activities required for the project.
  • Coordinate all aspects of field construction in compliance with the Contract Documents and in coordination/communication with all parties involved in the project including the Project Manager, property managers, maintenance managers, architects, design consultants, accounting staff, owners, and end-users.
  • Conduct progress review – assess project status and site conditions documenting and taking progress photos of ongoing and completed work. Document any approved deviations in as-builts and specifications.
  • Having an understanding of building concepts related to construction of the project. Proactively identify and communicate safety improvements and hazardous/potentially hazardous conditions to maintain a safe, healthy work environment to meet Cal-OSHA and company standards.
  • Builds and maintains positive working relationships with customers, design consultants, public agencies, subcontractors, and internal departments. Facilitates communication of company and project policies, procedures, and standards.
Skills & Experience Required:
  • 10+ years of Construction Superintendent experience in either: new construction, renovation and rehab, development and commercial, preferably experience in healthcare new construction and renovation sector.
  • Thorough knowledge of commercial construction contracts/laws. Have knowledge of municipal, environment and regulating agency requirements, codes, ADA requirements, and be able to implement all systems required to comply with those laws and requirements.
  • Familiarity with accepted building practices, means, methods, materials, techniques and mechanical, electrical, plumbing systems. Ability to track and manage subcontractor production and manpower.
  • Management abilities. Be a resource to the field crews and subcontractors to proactively resolve questions and issues to prevent delays or additional costs.
  • Constantly monitor workmanship for quality control and adherence to the contract documents.
  • Experienced in industry practices, processes, standards, technology, equipment methods, cost control, scheduling and their impact on project activities.
  • Proficient in using project management and scheduling software. (CPM scheduling).
  • Ability to handle sensitive and confidential information.
  • Highest ethical standards requirement.
