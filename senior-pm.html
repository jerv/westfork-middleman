Senior Project Manager

Senior Project Manager

West Fork Construction is seeking an individual who has 10 to 15+ years of construction experience successfully completing projects in the manner described below. This individual would possess the following core values:

Strong work ethic:

Absolute dedication, honoring commitments and willingness to put in the extra effort to ensure client satisfaction and project success.

Teamwork:

Collaborative spirit, sharing ideas and talents with team members, subcontractors, architects and owners. Support others when needed and ask for help and support when required.

Communication:

Honest, straight forward, clear, open communicator. Actively listen to and be respectful of other people’s ideas and points of view.

Integrity:

Deliver a quality project experience as promised. Possess high business and personal ethics. Treat all people fairly and with honesty.

Key Responsibilities:

The Sr. Project Manager is involved in all phases of project implementations and will lead and/or participate in the following activities:

  • Contract Administration - Ability to manage the financial and contractual side of large  projects: process contractor  pay applications, evaluate and process change orders, analyze and maintain tracking sheets and records, perform inspection walks with bank inspectors, inspect and approve work in place, document stored materials and monitor punch/warranty work.
  • Pre-construction Services -   Participate in the development of large projects in concert with the project team from conceptual stages through C D’s and permit.  Manage and coordinate our MEP team through this process,  providing estimates and constructability reviews to ensure a successful project.
  • Management, coordination, and inspection of contractor work while project is in progress.  Contribute feedback that might make work safer, cheaper, cleaner, lower impact or any other way improve the operational success of the project
  • Maintain project management logs (RFIs, changes, submittals, construction documents, FF&E, close-out)
  • Controlling and monitoring project budget & schedule to ensure work is completed on time and within budget.
  • Pricing exercises, generation of take-offs and quantities for work, administration of individual/special projects  and communicate with Owner,  Property Managers,  Architects, Design staff, Accounting Staff.
  • Builds and maintains positive working relationships with customers,, A/E representatives, public agencies, contractors, and other internal departments. Facilitates communication of company and project policies, procedures, and standards. 
Skills & Experience Required:
  • Bachelor’s degree preferably in construction management, construction/building science, engineering, business management or architecture and design or equivalent training and experience.
  • 10 – 15+ years Construction Management experience.
  • Experience in negotiated, design/ build work for  new construction, renovation and rehab development.  Must be able to collaborate  with owners, architects, engineers and end users to create a successfully project and maintain a positive relationship.
  • Ability to lead the Preconstruction Services phase of the project including estimating, Preconstruction scheduling, value engineering, MEP design and construct-ability analysis and Budget management.
  • Thorough knowledge of construction contracts/laws
  • Familiarity with accepted buildings practices, materials and techniques, knowledge of common and general code requirements, ADA requirements, mechanical systems and components, permitting processes and requirements, market pricing and lien waivers.
  • Demonstrated leadership abilities.
  • Proficient at  industry practices, processes, standards, technology, equipment methods, cost control, scheduling and their impact on project activities.
  • Proficient in using project management and estimating software.
  • Expert in creation of CPM scheduling utilizing Microsoft Project.
  • Ability to handle sensitive and confidential information required.
  • Highest ethical standards required.
